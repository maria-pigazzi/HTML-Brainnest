Tango dancing

I always loved it even though it's considered a dance for old people both in Italy and Germany 😥.
It was "invented" in Argentina around the start of the 20th century, but the basics were inspired by italian ballroom dances
and other mediterranean folk dances that immigrants had brought to the Americas.
If you wish you can dance it slowly and carefully, and that's why I prefer tango to other latin dances such as salsa or bachata.
