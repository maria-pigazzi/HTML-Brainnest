Book reading

Literature

I love classic literature. Anything almost from the 19th and 20th centuries works for me! At the moment I'm reading "La maladie de Sachs" by Winckler. I like, if I can, to read the books in their original language.
When they are translated there is always the risk that something that the author wanted to express will not be conveyed (especially wordplay is very difficult to translate).

Bookclubs

I am part of a bookclub here in Munich. We read a book every month and then discuss it in front of a nice warm drink!
